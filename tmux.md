
## Session

|Command|Description|
|-------|-----------|
|tmux new -s \<session name\>|New|
|tmux ls|List|
|tmux a -t \<session name\>|Attach|
|ctrl-b d|Detach|
|ctrl-b (|Previous|
|ctrl-b )|Next|
|ctrl-b s|Choose|
|ctrl-b $|Rename|
|tmux kill-session -t \<session name\>|Kill|
